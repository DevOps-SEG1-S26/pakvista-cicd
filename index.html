Why PakVista

Pakistan is a country of staggering contrasts — you can drive from the warm coastline of Karachi to glacier-fed lakes in the north in a single trip. PakVista distils five of the country’s most loved destinations into a clean, fast, and accessible guide so you can plan a route that matches your travel style.

Curated coverage

Every destination page focuses on three signature attractions, so you spend less time researching and more time deciding.

Built for travellers

Lightweight, JavaScript-free pages that load instantly on slow connections — useful when you’re on the road.

Honest descriptions

No filler text. Each section gives you a clear sense of what the place looks like, feels like, and is best known for.

Punjab

Lahore

City of Gardens

The cultural heart of Pakistan, famed for the Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, and the Shalimar Gardens — a UNESCO World Heritage trio.

Gilgit–Baltistan

Hunza Valley

Roof of the World

A high-altitude paradise framed by Karakoram giants, with Rakaposhi views, the turquoise Attabad Lake, and the centuries-old Baltit Fort.

Karachi

City of Lights

Pakistan’s vibrant port metropolis — Clifton Beach by sundown, Mohatta Palace by day, and the Pakistan Maritime Museum in between.
